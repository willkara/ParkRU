About This Site

Who are you?

I'm Will.

Why did you make this site?

At some point, we've all gone through the struggle to find a parking space at Rutgers. This site will hopefully ease your struggles of finding a parking space where you can actually park.

How did you make it?

I used some of the publicly available information that Rutgers has and then just threw it in a blender and out came this website. This site will be constantly improved. If you can think of any features you'd like, please let me know!

The second link is NOT an official Rutgers Export. Rutgers has a knack for saying that they have the information available, but not actually making it available easily (or at all). If any of the information on this page is wrong or could be improved in anyway, please e-mail me at willkara@oit.rutgers.edu. This site is not affiliated with Rutgers in anyway. It's also not my fault if you get a ticket... read the signs.

I'll be updating a change-log located at my blog. It'll have the most up to date info on upcoming features and ideas.
